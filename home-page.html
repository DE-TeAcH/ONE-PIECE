- One~Piece :

STORY :

- It's an anime that was started over 24 years ago and has yet to be finished by EITCHIRO ODA; one of the best mangaka in world.
The least thing we could say about it, is that OP is the best anime and manga ever made, It's about a crew made of a captain and 10 crewmates; each one of them is seeking for their own dream, but episode after episode you'll know that their final dream is making the way clear to their captain MONKEY~D~LUFFY to became the king of the pirates.

REMARK : Being The King of The Pirates isn't LUFYY's dream, it's just the only thing can help him to reach his trully dream which we don't know.
